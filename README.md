## Farm Management Platforms

Digital platforms for planning, monitoring, and managing farm operations.

- [Agworld](https://www.agworld.com/) — Farm management software for planning and tracking agricultural operations.
- [Climate FieldView](https://climate.com/) — Digital agriculture platform for data-driven farm management.
- [Granular](https://granular.ag/) — Farm management platform for operational and financial planning.
- [Trimble Agriculture](https://agriculture.trimble.com/) — Precision agriculture and farm management solutions.

## Precision Agriculture

Technologies that optimize inputs and improve efficiency through data and automation.

- [John Deere Operations Center](https://operationscenter.deere.com/) — Platform for analyzing farm data and optimizing equipment use.
- [Raven Precision](https://ravenind.com/) — Precision agriculture technologies for guidance, control, and data management.
- [Topcon Agriculture](https://www.topconpositioning.com/solutions/agriculture) — Precision positioning and control systems for farming.

## Robotics & Automation

Automation technologies and robotic systems for agricultural operations.

- [Blue River Technology](https://bluerivertechnology.com/) — Machine learning-based precision spraying and automation systems.
- [Naïo Technologies](https://www.naio-technologies.com/) — Autonomous robots for weeding and farming operations.
- [SwarmFarm Robotics](https://www.swarmfarm.com/) — Autonomous farming robots designed for scalable field operations.

## Remote Sensing & Drones

Aerial and satellite-based technologies for monitoring crops and land conditions.

- [DJI Agriculture](https://www.dji.com/agriculture) — Drone solutions for crop spraying and aerial monitoring.
- [Planet Labs](https://www.planet.com/) — Satellite imagery platform for agricultural monitoring and analytics.
- [Sentera](https://sentera.com/) — Drone-based imaging and analytics for precision agriculture.

## Soil & Crop Intelligence

Tools and platforms for understanding soil conditions, crop health, and environmental factors.

- [CropX](https://www.cropx.com/) — Soil sensing and analytics platform for irrigation optimization.
- [FarmLogs](https://farmlogs.com/) — Farm data platform for tracking crop performance and field conditions.
- [Taranis](https://taranis.com/) — AI-powered crop intelligence platform using aerial imagery.

## Supply Chain & Market Platforms

Platforms that connect producers with markets, logistics, and financial systems.

- [Bushel](https://www.bushelpowered.com/) — Digital platform for grain supply chain management and communication.
- [Indigo Ag](https://www.indigoag.com/) — Marketplace and sustainability platform for agriculture.
- [FarmLead](https://farmlead.com/) — Online grain marketplace connecting buyers and sellers.

## Sustainability & Climate

Technologies and initiatives focused on sustainable agriculture and climate resilience.

- [Agreena](https://agreena.com/) — Carbon farming platform supporting regenerative agriculture practices.
- [Cool Farm Tool](https://coolfarmtool.org/) — Tool for measuring greenhouse gas emissions in agriculture.
- [Regrow](https://www.regrow.ag/) — Platform for monitoring and verifying sustainable farming practices.
